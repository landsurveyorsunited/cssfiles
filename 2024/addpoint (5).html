What is this tool for?

Making a blog post is one thing. Geolocating your blog post to show up in the global community points map is entirely on another level. Share your FOTD or Today's Office or just show us where you surveyed. Use this experimental tool to simply create the HTML needed to make your Point Post which will show up inside the Surveyor Atlas and inside the Surveying Community Globe.

1️⃣ Add the location information for your point and grab a Point Badge for your post.

2️⃣ Generate the HTML code needed to add to your post for geolocation.

3️⃣Add a Point to a new Point Post inside LSU by copying and pasting the HTML into your post >>>.
