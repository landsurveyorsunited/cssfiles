Aston Martin V8

The new Aston Martin V8 Vantage was first seen at the Geneva Motor Show in 2005. The two seat, two door coupe has a bonded aluminium structure for strength and lightness. The car is powered by a 4.3 L quad-cam 32-valve V8 which produces 380 hp (283 kW) at 7000 rpm and 302 ft·lbf (409 N·m) at 5000 rpm. This engine is unique to Aston Martin and features race-style dry-sump lubrication, which enables it to be mounted low to lower the centre of gravity. The car can accelerate to 60 mph (97 km/h) in 4.9 seconds.

The V8 Vantage will cost £79,000 or €104,000 when it goes on sale towards the end of summer 2005 and is intended to compete with the Porsche 911. [source]
