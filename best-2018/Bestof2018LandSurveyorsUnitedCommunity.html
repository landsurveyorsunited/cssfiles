For Listing Your Company Website for Better Search Engine Rankings

Do you want to show up higher in search when people google "Land Surveyors Near Me"  We've enlisted the help of jbirds.co web services for Land Surveyors to help us build a one of a kind tool to help land surveying company rank higher in local search!

Visit http://nearme.landsurveyorsunited.org and Add your surveying company website today!  The more information you include the better your rankings will be.

* Currently we mostly have surveying websites in the USA so if you are based outside of the US, take advantage of the opportunity by listing your companies in those categories first!

Finally, We have a New Platform for 2019 to Migrate to

This has been a long time in the making.  We are almost ready to move to the new and improved, mobile friendly platform.  When it is ready, it will replace the current website sitting at http://landsurveyorsunited.org/  What we really need are a few end of the year donations to the community to make it happen!

Thurs.21st- Survey Earth in a Day 7

Survey Earth in a Day is a unique global surveying event in which any land surveyor or surveying student/professor with access to GNSS equipment can participate from their own location. So, the aim in so far as the participants are concerned is simply to survey the entire surface of the earth as a global community in a single day. Our terrestrial ground control points compiled will give us a network map which can be overlaid on top of any web map and at some places will show us greater accuracy than can be achieved with cell phone GPS..
